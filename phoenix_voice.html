ASSEMBLY — VOICE MODULE

STEP 7: VOICE AI INTEGRATION (NEW)

  1. Mount ESP32-S3 module on secondary PCB area
  2. Connect ESP32 to STM32 via UART (PA9/PA10)
  3. Wire I2S microphones:
    • SPH0645 primary mic → collar area (facing mouth)
    • INMP441 secondary → opposite side (noise reference)
  4. Install bone conduction transducer at collar, skin-contact side
  5. Connect MAX98357A amplifier between ESP32 and transducer
  6. Flash ESP32 with Phoenix Voice firmware via USB
  7. Load wake word model + response templates to W25Q256
  8. Test voice activation: "Hey Phoenix" → "I'm listening"

MIC PLACEMENT

PRIMARY MIC: Inside collar, ~2cm from edge, facing upward toward mouth. Protected by acoustic mesh fabric.

SECONDARY MIC: Opposite collar side, facing outward. Captures ambient noise for cancellation.

BONE CONDUCTION PLACEMENT

Mount transducer at collar base, positioned to contact mastoid bone area behind ear when collar sits naturally. Silicone pad ensures skin contact and comfort.
